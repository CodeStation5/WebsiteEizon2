What we do

Located in Nanaimo British Columbia serving Nanaimo Victoria. Eizon digital is here help with your Network, Security Requirements and Electronic Media Integration.

CCTV Installs

Closed circuit video monitoring and documentation of movement and activity in areas of your business help to ensure you are secure and limiting liability or losses. Incident documentation helps to ensure you have video documentation of issues that can effect your bottom line. There are many choices and options when it comes to video documentation.

Communications Network

Your business communications network is our specialty. We can handle anything from video conferencing, low voltage data network installation, camera, security integration, media rooms, phone, CCTV integration and automation. Ask us about anything electrical as our experience on many projects makes us a one stop shop.

Retail Merchant Equipment installation

POS, Credit card terminal and printer installation, trouble shooting and modem, data link install and repair are within our capability
